About SandwichNotes

A small note taking app built around one idea, that keeping things simple makes them easier to use.

What it is

SandwichNotes is a lightweight note taking program. You write a note, give it a place in a stack, and it stays put until you need it again. There are no complicated menus to learn and nothing to configure before you start.

Why the name

A good sandwich is just a few simple layers put together in the right order. Notes work the same way. SandwichNotes lets you stack related notes together so everything stays organized without extra folders or tags getting in the way.

Who it's for

Anyone who wants a place to jot things down and trust that it will still be there later. Students, writers, and anyone keeping a running list will feel at home.

How it's built

Fast

Opens quickly and saves as you type, so you never lose a thought.

Private

Notes are stored on your device, not sent anywhere else.

Simple

Only the features you need, nothing extra to get in the way.
